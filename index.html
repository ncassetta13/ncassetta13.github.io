Welcome to UNC Chess

The Home of the #1 Collegiate Chess Team

Welcome!

We are home to the top rated chess team in all of American Colleigate Chess.

We are open to all skill and experience levels.

Regardless of your abilities, we have a place for you to play.

It's Free!

There is no cost to play or join our club. We are funded by donors and the school, making our club free for everyone.

Events!

Tournaments: We host tournaments for all ages and skill levels, usually on the last Saturday of every month.

Open Play: Weekly free play time is always available. Open to everyone.

Workshops: We also hold learning sessions for newer players, or anyone that wants to learn the basics!
