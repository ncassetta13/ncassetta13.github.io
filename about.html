About UNC Chess

Who we are

We are the University of North Carolina Chess Team. We are a school approved club who host events for not only students, but the public as well.

We are open to all ages, cultures, and skill levels. Even if you've never seen a chess board before, we have people here who can get you started on your chess journey!

For more advanced players, we do have a travel tournament team with a limited number of roster spots. We are currently ranked #1 among all colleges in the United States!

The best part about us is that we are a non-profit organization, meaning there is no cost to those who participate. We do encourage donations, but they are certainly not necessary.
